What causes ED?

Erectile dysfunction is the inability to achieve or maintain an erection during sexual activity. This common condition occurs when the penile erection chambers are unable to generate and maintain the necessary high pressure for a fully hard erection. There are several causes of ED, including age, stress, obesity, heart disease, high blood pressure, mental health struggles, certain medications, smoking, and more.
