What is Tadalafil?

Tadalafil (Generic Cialis®) is an FDA-approved effective prescription medication for treating erectile dysfunction. Tadalafil contains the same active ingredient as Cialis® and is up to 90% cheaper. It also has longer-lasting effects than Viagra® (Sildenafil), staying active in your body for up to 24-36 hours. Tadalafil is available for as-needed (5mg, 10mg and 20mg doses) or to be taken daily (2.5mg and 5mg doses).

How does Tadalafil work?

PDE5 Enzyme

Tadalafil is an ED pill that works by blocking the PDE5 enzyme.

More blood flow

When PDE5 is blocked, blood flow is increased to the penis.
