What is Finasteride?

Finasteride, also known as Generic Propecia®, is an FDA-approved prescription medication used to treat male pattern baldness, which is the most common form of hair loss that affects millions of men worldwide. Finasteride 1mg is an oral pill taken once daily.

How does finasteride work?

Stops DHT

Finasteride blocks the production of DHT, which weakens hair follicles and accelerates hair loss.

Grows hair

Finasteride is clinically provent to support hair regrowth over a period of 3-6 months.

Makes you look great

Hair growth is linked to increased self-esteem and confidence. Say hello to more than just new hair.
